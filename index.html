Frequently asked questions

How long does business registration take?+

Most business registrations are completed within a week, depending on the Registrar General's Department processing time and how quickly required documents are provided.

Can you print and deliver, or just design?+

Both — flyers, banners, brochures, and certificates can be designed and printed, not just sent to you as a file.

Do you work with clients outside Accra?+

Yes. Design, web development, and cybersecurity coaching are handled remotely for clients anywhere in Ghana. Physical paperwork and ID applications are handled in person in Accra.

Can you build a website for my business?+

Yes. Websites are built from scratch, optimized for mobile and search engines, and delivered with clear instructions for updates.

What does cybersecurity coaching cover?+

Practical, hands-on sessions covering password hygiene, phishing awareness, device security, and safe browsing habits for individuals and small businesses.

Do you only work in person?+

No. Technical support, website work, cybersecurity teaching, and most design work can be done fully remotely. Installations (CCTV, dash cam) and printing naturally need to happen in person.
